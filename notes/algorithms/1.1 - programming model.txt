1.1 - Programming Model

primitive data types:
 - int
 - double
 - boolean
 - char

comparisons:
 - equal (==)
 - not equal (!=)
 - less than (<)
 - less than or equal (<=)
 - greater than (>)
 - greater than or equal (>=)

other primitive types:
 - long (64 bit integers)
 - short (16 bit integers)
 - char (16 bit characters)
 - byte (8 bit integers)
 - float (32 bit single precision real numbers)
