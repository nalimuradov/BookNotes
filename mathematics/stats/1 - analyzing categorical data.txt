1 - Analyzing Categorical Data

identifying individuals:
 - individuals of a data set aren't only people, rather they can be products
	- coffee shop with drink data set will have the DRINKS as the individuals

categorical (aka quantitative) variables:
 - say the drinks include info like type, calories, sugars, and caffeine:
	- type can be either HOT or COLD (categorical)
	- calories, sugars, and caffeine can be any number (not categorical)

mean, median, mode, midrange:
 - mean: average
 - median: order and find middle value
 - mode: most common value
 - midrange: average of highest and lowest value

conditional distribution:
 - distribution of X given Y
 	- distribution of % correct given that students studied more than 60 minutes
